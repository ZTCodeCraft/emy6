Places to Visit

Set sail to paradise and explore stunning destinations that promise unforgettable adventures and breathtaking views.

Visit islands in Greece

Discover the magic of Greece as you visit enchanting islands, each offering unique charm, rich history, and pristine beaches.

Zakynthos

Known for its Shipwreck Beach and loggerhead turtles, visitors can swim in turquoise waters, explore Blue Caves, and sample local cuisine.

Santorini

Known for its stunning sunsets and blue-domed churches, visitors can explore volcanic beaches, taste local wines, and wander through cliffside villages.

Lefkada

Known for its turquoise waters and windsurfing spots, visitors can explore Porto Katsiki Beach, hike to waterfalls, and sail around nearby islets.

Kefalonia

Renowned for its stunning beaches and dramatic landscapes, visitors can explore Melissani Cave, hike Mount Ainos, and relax in picturesque villages.

Paros

Famous for its white marble and vibrant nightlife, visitors can explore picturesque villages, windsurf on golden beaches, and visit historic churches.

Siphnos

Renowned for its pottery tradition and tranquil atmosphere, visitors can explore quaint villages, hike scenic trails, and relax on uncrowded beaches.

Naxos

Known for its ancient ruins and sandy beaches, visitors can explore the Portara, hike Mount Zas, and windsurf in crystal-clear waters.

Milos

Renowned for its diverse landscapes and Venus de Milo statue, visitors can swim in volcanic hot springs, explore catacombs, and relax on colorful beaches.
