Payments are made to the bank account of the company / tour operator after booking and obtaining an individual contract for organized tourist trip. Mandatory fees as skippers fee, service pack, internet and damage waiver are to be paid in cash to the captain upon embarcation. Payment of a deposit of 50% within 5 days after booking and confirmation of the invitation to participate in the party Final payment up to the full amount within 30 days before departure.

The yacht is designed for 6 people. There are 3 cabins, each with a double bed.

You can only rent this yacht with a professional captain on board. At the same time, keep in mind that food and cabin for the master are not included in the price of the boat.

We offer early check-in only upon request and at an additional cost. Early registrations usually have limited availability, so please let us know in advance so we can reserve a seat for you.

Here, much depends on how often you want to dock at marinas and small towns along the coast. In general, however, we recommend dishes that can be prepared quickly and serve the entire crew. It is best to plan your main menu before traveling and have backup plan if things don't go according to plan and you need to improvise. Definitely buy water for the marina trip before you set sail. Rely on at least 1 liter of water per person per day. Use local fresh ingredients – fresh seafood or local fruits and vegetables are a great addition to the menu.

When you accept the boat, take copies of the documents you sent to the charter company (ID, booking confirmation, insurance).
